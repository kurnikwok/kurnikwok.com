AI-shaping intelligence evidence

Evidence for the mediated-shaping approach through AI-shaping intelligence

The published shaping-capability evidence route supports the mediated-shaping approach through AI-shaping intelligence: the reusable shaping capability developed vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence, then extended that shaped capability into broader project-managing shaped intelligence, while implementation-level review remains protected-stage.

Instance distinction

Both are instances, but not the same instance

AI-shaping intelligence and project-managing shaped intelligence are both capability instances, but they play different evidence roles. AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping-capability instance; project-managing shaped intelligence is the primary published domain-facing capability-evidence instance.

How this differs from the project-managing evidence page

This page explains the evidence position for the reusable shaping capability. The project-managing shaped intelligence page explains the primary published domain-facing capability-evidence instance in project and BAU IT support work.

How this differs from the concept page

The AI-shaping intelligence concept page defines the term. This page explains the evidence distinction so the concept page does not also carry the evidence architecture.

Current evidence and planned directions remain separate

The current public evidence shows bounded domain extension from vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence into broader project-managing shaped intelligence. Cross-domain transfer remains a distinct evaluation question and does not become evidence merely because a future direction is named.
