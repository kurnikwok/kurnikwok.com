AI-shaping intelligence evidence

Evidence for mediated shaping through AI-shaping intelligence

The published shaping-capability evidence route supports mediated shaping through AI-shaping intelligence: the reusable shaping capability developed vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence, then extended that shaped capability into broader project-managing shaped intelligence, while implementation-level correspondence remains protected-stage.

Instance distinction

Both are instances, but not the same instance

AI-shaping intelligence and project-managing shaped intelligence are both capability instances, but they play different evidence roles. AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping-capability instance; project-managing shaped intelligence is the primary published domain-facing capability-evidence instance.

What this page evidences publicly

Evidence focusPublic-safe signalBoundary
AI-shaping intelligenceThe reusable shaping capability through which mediated shaping develops and renews shaped intelligence.Public pages do not disclose private control surfaces, prompts, schemas, validation mechanics or route controls.
Self-shaping capabilityThe shaping pattern can be developed, audited, improved and renewed rather than being a one-off prompt or static template.This explains one capability surface in mediated shaping, not implementation guidance.

Domain extensionVendor-evaluating shaped intelligence extended into project-managing shaped intelligence within a related project-work family.Domain extension does not prove unrestricted domain transfer.

How this differs from the project-managing evidence page

This page explains the evidence position for the reusable shaping capability. The project-managing shaped intelligence page explains the primary published domain-facing capability-evidence instance in project and BAU IT support work.

How this differs from the concept page

The AI-shaping intelligence concept page defines the term. This page explains the evidence distinction so the concept page does not also carry the evidence architecture.

Domain extension and transfer evaluation

AI-shaping intelligence also matters because the capability architecture has demonstrated bounded domain extension and makes cross-domain transfer separately evaluable. Extension and transfer are related, but they are not the same public claim.

Capability relationshipPublic meaningCurrent status and boundary
Domain transfer / transferabilityEvaluating target-domain rematerialisation of the shaping pattern in a different work domain; transferability is the evaluation property, not proof that transfer has occurred.Planned protected-stage evaluation direction only: investment-evaluating shaped intelligence is not a current public capability-evidence claim, financial advice or implementation authority.
Domain extension / extensibilityActual widening within a related work family; extensibility is the evaluation property concerning whether such widening can occur.Current public evidence shows vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence extending into project-managing shaped intelligence. A later portfolio-managing extension is possible only if investment-evaluating shaped intelligence is first successfully materialised and evidenced.
