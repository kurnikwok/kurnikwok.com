Why this page exists

One route across the public material

The website explains the category in plain language, the papers provide the DOI-backed public release, and the protected-stage page explains when public material is not enough for the next question.

This page keeps those roles together so the public material is read as a bounded evaluation route rather than as implementation guidance or a sales page.

Boundary

Boundary

The public pages and papers support category understanding and bounded public-stage capability evaluation only. They do not provide implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer or commercial-use rights.
