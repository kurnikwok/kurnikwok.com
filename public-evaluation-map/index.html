Why this page exists

One route across the public material

Private paper-authoring, Published paper-authoring, Public web-developing and Private knowledge-publishing shaped intelligence refine the publication architecture through controlled cross-surface publication co-evolution. Private papers develop complete coherence and practitioner codification; published papers formalise the public basis; the public website clarifies and makes that basis inspectable; and the private knowledge surface synthesises authorised material for digestible practitioner use and clarity testing. One instance is active at a time, owner-level reconciliation controls authority, and no change propagates automatically.

Keeping those roles together prevents reciprocal refinement from blurring evidence status: clearer website explanation is not, by itself, stronger capability evidence or a sales claim.

Boundary

Where the public route stops

The public pages and papers support category understanding and bounded public-stage capability evaluation only. They do not provide implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer or commercial-use rights.
