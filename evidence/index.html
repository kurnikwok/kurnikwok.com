Evidence overview

AI shaping evidence starts with visible work-burden shift

Start with a simple, non-redacted direct-shaping website instance, then follow the stronger mediated-shaping evidence route through AI-shaping intelligence to project-managing shaped intelligence and the public evaluation boundary.

Evidence architecture

A simple direct instance and a separate mediated evidence route

The evidence layer separates a simple direct-shaping website instance from the stronger mediated-shaping evidence route. Web-developing shaped intelligence provides a non-redacted direct-shaping instance of work-burden shift on this website. Separately, AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability in mediated shaping; it developed vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence and extended that capability into project-managing shaped intelligence, the primary published domain-facing capability-evidence instance.

The current capability evidence paper acknowledges the website-specific direct-shaping route but does not include this instance in its structured evidence package. The DOI-backed paper remains the formal public source for the published mediated-shaping project evidence route through AI-shaping intelligence. The website-specific instance does not evidence AI-shaping-intelligence domain transfer; implementation-level correspondence remains protected-stage.

Evidence in brief

The three diagrams answer three different questions. Web-developing shaped intelligence makes the burden-shift mechanism easy to see. AI-shaping intelligence shows the reusable shaping capability. Project-managing shaped intelligence shows the stronger, more complex domain-facing evidence instance in project and BAU IT support work.

The diagrams give public-safe before/after views. They show observable capability relationships and burden shift without disclosing implementation mechanics, providing deployment assurance, proving reproducibility or authorising method transfer.

What would not be enough evidence?

Useful one-off AI output, a polished demo, a private implementation description by itself, or an illustrative transfer map without visible burden-shift evidence would not be enough. The public question is narrower: whether the available evidence shows reviewable work-burden shift under human authority, while leaving implementation-level review to protected-stage discussion.

Why this index is separate from the papers index

The papers index is for publication access and citation. This evidence overview is for website navigation: it tells readers which evidence page to use before they open the formal capability evidence paper.

Why this index is not a substitute paper

This overview may route to newer website-specific public evidence, but it does not replace the formal papers or present that newer evidence as if it were already included in them. The capability evidence paper remains the formal source for its published evidence set.

Public boundary

This page supports bounded public-stage capability evaluation only. It is not implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer or commercial-use rights. Implementation-level review belongs in Protected-stage discussion.
