Reader guide

Public evidence map

A concise map of how the public capability evidence should be read without converting it into implementation guidance, method transfer or deployment assurance.

Purpose of this map

The capability evidence paper tests whether visible before/after transitions show work-burden shift from the responsible person to project-managing shaped intelligence while judgement, approval, escalation, communication and real-world action remain human-owned.

This page is a public reader map only. It does not add implementation detail or expand the claim beyond bounded public-stage capability evaluation.

Evidence-reading map

Burden areaPublic evidence questionHuman-owned boundaryExcluded stronger claim
ReconstructionDoes the shaped capability reduce repeated rebuilding of the work basis?Human review checks source basis, assumptions and use of context.Reproducibility proof or method transfer.
Sequencing and trackingDoes repeated work become easier to continue across pauses and updates?Human decision-making remains responsible for priorities and action.Autonomous project execution.
Dependency and risk framingDoes the evidence show reviewable surfacing of blockers, dependencies and risk implications?Human judgement decides what matters and what action follows.Deployment assurance or product-performance assurance.
Domain-practice applicationDoes the output reflect relevant project/support practice expectations?Human authority determines whether the practice framing is appropriate.Professional-advice substitution.
Subject-context reasoningDoes the capability use project, issue, stakeholder or support context in a useful and bounded way?Human review remains responsible for disclosure, judgement and approval.Unbounded access to protected method or source material.
State preservation and resumptionDoes the work resume without the person rebuilding everything from scratch?Human ownership of decisions, escalation and real-world action remains explicit.Implementation-ready system design.
Communication preparationDoes shaped intelligence prepare reviewable communication material?The human decides whether to send, approve or escalate.Independent external communication.

Permitted public conclusion

The public material can support category understanding, initial capability evaluation and whether protected-stage discussion is justified.

Not permitted from public material alone

The public material does not support implementation guidance, method transfer, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance or commercial use rights.
