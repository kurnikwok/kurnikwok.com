Concept page

AI-shaping intelligence renews the shaping pattern and produces shaped intelligence

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable meta-capability that combines self-shaping capability and domain-shaping capability: it builds, audits, improves and renews its own shaping pattern, and uses that pattern to produce shaped intelligence under human direction.

Plain-first guide

Think of the dual shaping capability

This page is about the reusable capability that both improves the shaping pattern and uses that pattern to produce shaped intelligence. It is not the same thing as one AI answer, one prompt or one output.

For cold readers, the useful distinction is this: AI-shaping intelligence combines self-shaping capability and domain-shaping capability; shaped intelligence is the resulting domain-facing capability used in recurring work.

Self-shaping capabilityBuilds, audits, improves and renews the shaping pattern.
Domain-shaping capabilityUses that pattern to produce domain-facing shaped intelligence.

Reader route: use this page for the concept, then use the glossary or formal paper when the full source is needed.

Definition

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable meta-capability that combines self-shaping capability and domain-shaping capability.

In plain English: it improves the reusable work pattern itself and uses that pattern to form shaped intelligence, so the person is not re-explaining the whole job each time. It shapes the work basis and resumption route; it does not change the model’s trained weights.

Both capability surfaces remain controlled: AI can help propose, refine and apply the pattern, but human direction and acceptance remain explicit.

Why this term is needed

The important capability is not only producing an answer, and not only maintaining a pattern in isolation. It is renewing the reusable shaping pattern and using that pattern to produce shaped intelligence that allows future AI-assisted work to continue rather than restart from a rebuilt prompt.

How it differs from adjacent ideas

Adjacent ideaDistinction
A promptA prompt can trigger an output. AI-shaping intelligence renews the pattern and uses it to produce shaped intelligence that makes repeated work grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable.
An agentAn agent framing can suggest independent action. AI-shaping intelligence is bounded by human authority and does not claim to own judgement, communication or real-world action.
A document templateA template can standardise format. AI-shaping intelligence also carries continuity, review logic, unresolved-decision handling, shaped-intelligence formation and improvement of the shaping pattern itself.

Example

A recurring project-management support capability that preserves the source basis, review state, stakeholder context, unresolved decisions, risks, dependencies and next-action logic across pauses and updates.

Non-example

A static checklist that does not preserve context, improve the work pattern or reduce the burden of rebuilding the work basis each time.

Assessment signal

It is assessed through whether self-shaping improves the shaping pattern, whether domain-shaping produces usable shaped intelligence, whether the recurring burden of the work actually shifts, and whether any claimed domain extension or domain transfer is properly bounded and evidenced.

Domain extensibility and transferability

AI-shaping intelligence also matters because the shaping pattern can be evaluated for domain extensibility and domain transferability. They are related, but they are not the same claim.

Domain extension means widening shaped intelligence within a related work family, where the earlier shaped capability becomes a subset of a broader shaped capability. The current public evidence shows this path: vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence extended into project-managing shaped intelligence because vendor evaluation is a subset of broader project-management work.

Domain transfer means evaluating whether the shaping pattern can be rematerialised for a different work domain. Investment-evaluating shaped intelligence is a planned domain-transfer direction into investment portfolio management, not a current public capability-evidence claim and not part of the DOI-backed papers.

Domain extensibility and domain transferability are evaluation properties; domain extension and domain transfer are the actual evidenced or evaluated moves. The distinction matters: proven domain extension does not prove domain transfer, and public transferability framing does not authorise implementation, protected-method transfer or commercial reliance.
