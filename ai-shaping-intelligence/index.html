AI-shaping intelligence

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable meta-capability that builds, improves and renews the shaping pattern for productive AI work.

Definition. AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable meta-capability that builds, improves and renews its own shaping pattern so that shaped intelligence can carry more recurring work burden in a domain.

Self-shaping role

The term points to more than a prompt, a workflow or a single artefact. It describes the intelligence that shapes the operating pattern itself: the source basis, review routes, continuity structures, domain-practice framing, evidence boundaries and repeatable methods that let work continue instead of being rebuilt from scratch.

Relationship to shaped intelligence

AI-shaping intelligence produces and improves shaped intelligence. Shaped intelligence is closer to the working domain: it helps carry a specific recurring burden, such as project-managing support, issue framing, risk and dependency tracking, or review-ready communication preparation.

Boundary

The concept does not claim independent agency, replacement of responsible people or implementation transfer. It describes a controlled pattern in which shaped capability can be reviewed and directed by a person.
