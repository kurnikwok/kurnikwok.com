Concept page

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability in the mediated-shaping approach

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability used in the mediated-shaping approach to develop shaped intelligence for a target work domain. Through complementary self-shaping and domain-shaping capabilities, it develops, audits, improves and renews its own shaping pattern and uses that pattern to develop shaped intelligence under human direction.

Mediated shaping is not a simple-versus-complex ladder. It is justified when separately reusable shaping capability, repeated rematerialisation, extension or cross-domain transfer evaluation warrants the added architecture; otherwise the direct-shaping approach may be the more reliable fit.

Shaping capability

Self-shaping and domain-shaping work together

This page is about the reusable capability used in the mediated-shaping approach: it develops and renews the shaping pattern and uses that pattern to develop shaped intelligence. It is not an individual AI answer, prompt or output.

The direct-shaping approach establishes shaped intelligence directly. The mediated-shaping approach establishes it through AI-shaping intelligence. AI-shaping intelligence combines self-shaping capability and domain-shaping capability within the mediated approach.

Self-shaping capabilityDevelops, audits, improves and renews the shaping pattern.
Domain-shaping capabilityUses that pattern to develop domain-facing shaped intelligence for a target work domain.

Definition

AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability used in the mediated-shaping approach to develop, govern or renew shaped intelligence through complementary self-shaping and domain-shaping capabilities.

In plain English: it develops and renews the shaping pattern and uses that pattern to develop shaped intelligence for a target work domain. At the shaping stage, it arranges the domain-practice standards, subject-context reasoning, work-state preservation and work-resumption logic that the resulting shaped intelligence will carry into recurring work, so the person does not have to rebuild the work basis each time. It does not change the model’s trained weights.

Both capability surfaces remain controlled: AI can help propose, refine and apply the pattern, but human direction and acceptance remain explicit.

Why this term is needed

The important capability is not only producing an answer, and not only maintaining a pattern in isolation. It is renewing the reusable shaping pattern and using that pattern to develop shaped intelligence that allows future AI-assisted work to continue rather than restart from a rebuilt prompt.

How it differs from adjacent ideas

How it differs from adjacent ideas
Adjacent ideaDistinction
A promptA prompt can trigger an output. AI-shaping intelligence develops and renews the shaping pattern and uses it to develop shaped intelligence that supports grounded, practice-guided, subject-aware, reviewable, repeatable and resumable work.
An agentAn agent framing can suggest independent action. AI-shaping intelligence is bounded by human authority and does not claim to own judgement, communication or real-world action.
A document templateA template can standardise format. AI-shaping intelligence develops and renews the shaping pattern and uses it to develop shaped intelligence that carries domain-practice standards, subject-context reasoning, work-state preservation, unresolved-matter handling and work-resumption logic into recurring work.

Example

Developing vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence as the first successful wedge and then extending that shaped capability into project-managing shaped intelligence under human direction.

Non-example

A static prompt, checklist or template that cannot audit or renew the shaping pattern or use it to develop shaped intelligence.

Concept signal

The concept is relevant when the shaping problem itself requires a reusable capability that can develop and renew the shaping pattern, use it to develop or extend shaped intelligence, or evaluate target-domain rematerialisation in a different work domain. AI-shaping intelligence names that mediating shaping capability while human authority remains explicit.
