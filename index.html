What AI shaping addresses

Useful AI output is not the same as productive AI work. The central question is whether recurring work burden can shift to shaped intelligence while judgement, approval and real-world action remain human-owned.

Grounded Work remains tied to source basis, context and review state.
Reviewable Human judgement, approval and disclosure remain explicit.
Repeatable The work pattern can recur without rebuilding the whole basis.
Resumable Work can continue after pause, handover or new information.
