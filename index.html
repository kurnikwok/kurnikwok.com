AI shaping

Make AI carry more of the recurring work burden, not just answer the prompt

General-purpose AI can produce useful answers and drafts. The harder problem is whether more recurring work burden can move from the responsible person to shaped intelligence across domain practice and subject context, and across work state and resumption.

AI shaping is the operating-model discipline for that shift. AI shaping establishes shaped intelligence through either the direct-shaping approach or the mediated-shaping approach. Shaped intelligence carries more of the recurring burden; the resulting productive AI work is grounded, practice-guided, subject-aware, reviewable, repeatable and resumable under human direction.

A familiar work pain

Imagine a real work bundle: ticket notes, emails, screenshots, vendor replies, stakeholder questions and a half-finished status update. Ordinary AI can summarise parts of it, but the person still has to rebuild the work basis before the output can be trusted or continued.

AI shaping shapes the work pattern and arranges a reusable work basis so shaped intelligence can carry more domain-practice and subject-context burden, and more work-state and resumption burden, into recurring work. It matters most when work continues across pauses, changing inputs, review cycles, handover or escalation; source basis is carried forward while judgement, approval and real-world action remain human-owned.

The practical shift: I direct, review and decide. Shaped intelligence carries more of the recurring work.
