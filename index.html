AI shaping

Make AI carry the work, not just answer the prompt

General-purpose AI can produce useful answers and drafts. Complex or extended work breaks when people must keep rebuilding context, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, decisions and next steps. AI shaping gives AI a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern and carries the work state forward, so work can be reviewed, resumed and reused under human direction.

The simple problem

Every new chat starts without the job

Ordinary AI use can help with one answer, summary or draft. The harder problem appears when work continues across pauses, changing inputs, review cycles, handover or escalation.

The person keeps carrying the repeated burden: reloading context, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, prior decisions, unresolved matters, review status and next action.

The simple mechanism

Shape the work pattern, carry the work state

Shaping gives AI the right role, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, review boundaries and output expectations for the work.

Resumption carries forward what happened, what changed, what remains unresolved, what needs review and what happens next.
