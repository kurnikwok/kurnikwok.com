Concept stack

Useful AI output is not the same as productive AI work. The central question is whether recurring work burden can shift to shaped intelligence while judgement, approval and real-world action remain human-owned.

AI shaping

The operating-model discipline for turning broad AI capability into productive AI work.

AI-shaping intelligence

The reusable meta-capability that builds, improves and renews the shaping pattern.

Shaped intelligence

The domain-facing reusable capability that carries recurring work burden in a work domain.

Productive AI work

Grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable work under human direction.

Work-burden shift

The visible before/after signal that more recurring work burden has moved to shaped intelligence.

Operating-model design

The broader design frame for roles, controls, boundaries and repeatable work patterns.

Public boundary

The published papers are for public category understanding and bounded public-stage capability evaluation. They do not provide implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer or commercial use rights.
