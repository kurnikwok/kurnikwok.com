Productive AI work

Productive AI work is grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable work under human direction, not merely useful AI output.

Definition. Productive AI work is AI-enabled work that is grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable under human direction.

Beyond useful output

A useful answer can still leave the person carrying the real work burden: rebuilding context, checking assumptions, sequencing actions, preserving state and translating output into safe communication or decision-ready material. Productive AI work is a higher threshold.

Four working properties

Grounded means the work remains tied to source basis, context and review state. Reviewable means human judgement, approval and disclosure remain explicit. Repeatable means the work pattern can recur without rebuilding the whole basis. Resumable means work can continue after pause, handover or new information.

Measurement frame

The practical question is whether the operating pattern reduces recurring burden while preserving responsible human control. That is why productive AI work is assessed through work-burden shift rather than through output quality alone.
