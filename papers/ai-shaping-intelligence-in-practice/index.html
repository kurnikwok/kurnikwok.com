Presents a public-safe capability-evidence instance where project-managing shaped intelligence carries more recurring project and BAU IT support work burden under human authority.

Abstract

This paper presents a public-safe capability-evidence instance from mediated shaping through AI-shaping intelligence: project-managing shaped intelligence in project and business-as-usual (BAU) IT support contexts. It tests whether visible before/after transitions show two-sided work-burden shift — domain-practice and subject-context burden, and work-state and resumption burden — moving from the responsible person to shaped intelligence across reconstruction, sequencing, drafting, tracking, dependency and risk framing, communication preparation and related project-state work while purpose, supplied context, judgement, review, approval, disclosure, escalation, communication, decisions and real-world action remain human-owned.

The structured public evidence package has two evidence streams and one illustrative transfer-evaluation map. Supporting domain-extensibility evidence comes from the actual extension from vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence into broader project-managing shaped intelligence. Primary capability evidence comes from project-managing shaped intelligence tested in real project and BAU IT support contexts. The illustrative transfer-evaluation map shows a planned protected-stage evaluation route for investment-evaluating shaped intelligence and possible portfolio-managing shaped intelligence; it does not create investment-domain capability evidence, provide financial advice or authorise action.

Section 10 is the controlling public evidence test; Appendix A provides lookup detail where a trace point needs checking. The permitted public conclusion is limited to initial capability evaluation and whether protected-stage discussion is justified. The paper is not implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer, financial advice, portfolio advice, investment-management assurance or a separate domain capability claim.

Public contribution

  • Presents public-safe evidence from project-managing shaped intelligence.
  • Tests visible before/after transitions in recurring work burden.
  • Keeps the human-authority boundary explicit.
  • Separates supporting evidence, primary capability evidence and illustrative transfer framing.

Public boundary

This paper supports bounded public-stage capability evaluation only. Use Section 10 as the controlling public evidence test. Implementation-level review belongs in protected-stage discussion.
