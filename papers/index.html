AI shaping papers

The public release separates the simple category idea from bounded capability evidence: shape a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern, carry the work state and keep human authority explicit.

Release status and responsible use

Both papers are DOI-backed public preprints published on Zenodo. They are intended for public category understanding and bounded public-stage capability evaluation.

Use the DOI records and the paper landing pages for citation, version and PDF access.

Use boundary

The public release does not provide implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer or commercial-use rights.
