About AI shaping and its development context

AI shaping is the public framing for turning useful AI output into productive AI work that is grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable under human direction. This page explains the practical development context and the author background only where it helps a reader assess credibility.

Credibility shortcut

Why this development context matters

The work sits at the intersection of IT support, project delivery, product ownership, software development and operating-model design. That matters because AI shaping is not presented as a generic prompting trick; it is framed as a work-system discipline for carrying context, standards, review state and resumption under human authority.

The public pages and papers are therefore structured to show the category, the evidence boundary and the protected-stage route without exposing implementation-level material.

The practical problem

Useful AI output is not the same as productive AI work that can be grounded, reviewable, repeatable, resumable and human-approved in real operating conditions.

AI shaping addresses the repeated reconstruction burden by shaping the work pattern and carrying the work state forward, so the work can continue rather than restart without removing human review, approval or action.

Public-stage boundary

The public material on this site supports category understanding and bounded public-stage capability evaluation only. It does not provide implementation guidance, deployment assurance, reproducibility proof, product-performance assurance, method transfer, protected operating material or commercial-use rights.

How the work developed

The work began with a practical investment decision-workflow problem: how to preserve why investment decisions were made, what was known at the time, what constraints applied, what changed afterwards and what should improve next time. The system was built around governed decision-making, evidence-linked reasoning, reviewable accountability and learning over time.

As general-purpose AI became more useful in the development process, the challenge changed. Better prompts helped, but prompt-by-prompt control still left too much recurring work with the person.

The practical need became a reusable pattern that AI could help build, refine and run under human direction. That is the shaping mechanism: AI is used not only to produce outputs, but also to improve the structured work basis that future AI work uses.

That development path produced the AI shaping framework, including AI-shaping intelligence and shaped intelligence. AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability: it builds, improves and renews the shaping pattern used to turn broad AI capability into domain-facing shaped intelligence. Shaped intelligence is the reusable work capability that carries more of a defined recurring work burden under human direction.

From first wedge to domain-bounded shaped intelligence

The path was not a straight jump from ordinary AI use to project-managing shaped intelligence. Early attempts made clear that project-managing shaped intelligence needed a stronger foundation, so the work was scaled back to AI-shaping intelligence first: the reusable shaping capability behind later shaped-intelligence instances.

The first successful wedge was vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence, which carried the burden of parsing vendor material, normalising options, assessing cost-benefit implications, framing risks and preparing decision material under human review. That shaped capability then extended into project-managing shaped intelligence because vendor evaluation was a subset of the broader project-management work pattern.

Project-managing shaped intelligence is the current public capability-evidence instance. Its significance is not that AI replaces human authority, judgement or action. Its significance is that more recurring project and BAU IT support work burden can be carried by shaped intelligence, while the responsible person directs, reviews, corrects, approves, decides, communicates and acts.

In practical terms, this changes the work pattern from repeatedly rebuilding the work basis to directing shaped intelligence that carries more of the tracking, sequencing, resumption and communication-preparation burden. The public evidence is framed around that shift.

Broader design direction

The broader design is domain-bounded rather than one all-purpose assistant in a single step. Project-managing shaped intelligence is the first public wedge: a shaped-intelligence instance that carries recurring project and BAU IT support work burden. The same AI-shaping intelligence is designed to support further shaped-intelligence instances, including software-developing shaped intelligence and investment decision-workflow shaped intelligence.

These further shaped-intelligence instances are design directions, not current public capability-evidence claims.

Investment decision-workflow shaped intelligence means a shaped-intelligence instance for investment decision workflows: preserving thesis, evidence, decision context, portfolio reality, review state, governance constraints and next-action logic. It does not mean self-authorised trade execution or replacement of the accountable decision-maker.
