About Kurni Kwok and AI shaping

Kurni Kwok develops AI-enabled work systems and operating-model concepts, with a focus on AI shaping: the discipline for turning general-purpose AI capability into productive AI work under human direction.

The practical problem

AI shaping addresses a practical problem: useful AI output is not the same as work that can be grounded, reviewed, repeated, resumed and approved in real operating conditions. In ordinary AI use, complex or extended work often forces the person to rebuild the AI work basis each time: context, source material, prior decisions, constraints, risks, review status and next action, often through a long or complex prompt. AI shaping focuses on carrying more of that work state forward so the work can continue rather than restart.

How the work developed

The work began with a practical investment-management problem: how to preserve why investment decisions were made, what was known at the time, what constraints applied, what changed afterwards and what should improve next time. The system was built around governed decision-making, evidence-linked reasoning, reviewable accountability and learning over time.

As general-purpose AI became more useful in the development process, the challenge changed. Better prompts helped, but prompt-by-prompt control still left too much recurring work with the person: rebuilding context, deciding the next instruction, preserving state, checking continuity and carrying work between sessions. The practical need became a better way to work with AI — not by rebuilding the whole prompt each time, but by shaping reusable intelligence that could propose, structure, check and resume work under human direction.

That development path produced the AI shaping framework, including AI-shaping intelligence and shaped intelligence. AI-shaping intelligence is the reusable shaping capability: it builds, improves and renews the shaping pattern used to turn broad AI capability into domain-facing shaped intelligence. Shaped intelligence is the reusable work capability that carries more of a defined recurring work burden under human direction.

From first wedge to domain-bounded shaped intelligence

The path was not a straight jump from ordinary AI use to project-managing shaped intelligence. Early attempts made clear that project-managing shaped intelligence needed a stronger foundation, so the work was scaled back to AI-shaping intelligence first: the reusable shaping capability behind later shaped-intelligence instances.

The first successful wedge was vendor-evaluating shaped intelligence, which carried the burden of parsing vendor material, normalising options, assessing cost-benefit implications, framing risks and preparing decision material under human review. That shaped capability then extended into project-managing shaped intelligence because vendor evaluation was a subset of the broader project-management work pattern.

Project-managing shaped intelligence is the current public capability-evidence instance. Its significance is not that AI replaces human authority, judgement or action. Its significance is that more of the recurring project-management work burden can be carried by shaped intelligence, while the responsible person directs, reviews, corrects, approves, decides, communicates and acts.

In practical terms, this changes the work pattern from repeatedly rebuilding the work basis — sources, status, prior decisions, open issues, risks, dependencies and next actions — to directing shaped intelligence that carries more of the tracking, sequencing, resumption and communication-preparation burden. The public evidence is framed around that shift: from isolated AI outputs towards reviewable, repeatable and resumable productive AI work.

Broader design direction

The broader design is domain-bounded rather than one all-purpose assistant in a single step. Project-managing shaped intelligence is the first public wedge: a shaped-intelligence instance that carries project-management work burden. The same AI-shaping intelligence is designed to support further shaped-intelligence instances, including software-developing shaped intelligence and investment-managing shaped intelligence.

Investment-managing shaped intelligence means a shaped-intelligence instance for investment-management work: preserving thesis, evidence, decision context, portfolio reality, review state, governance constraints and next-action logic. It does not mean self-authorised trade execution or replacement of the accountable decision-maker.
