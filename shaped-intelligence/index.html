Concept page

Shaped intelligence

Shaped intelligence is the domain-facing reusable capability that carries recurring work burden in a work domain under human authority.

Definition

Shaped intelligence is the domain-facing reusable capability that carries more of the recurring work burden in a work domain, including domain-practice application and subject-context reasoning, while human direction, judgement and action remain explicit.

In plain English: shaped intelligence is the AI capability after it has been structured for a specific kind of work.

Why this term is needed

The value is continuity with practice context. A person should not need to rebuild the whole work basis, domain-practice application, subject-context reasoning or next-action logic every time the work resumes, changes hands or receives new information.

How it differs from adjacent ideas

Adjacent ideaDistinction
Raw model capabilityRaw capability can answer broadly. Shaped intelligence is fitted to a domain pattern, source basis, review route, domain-practice standards, subject context and continuity burden.
A generated artefactA generated artefact is an output. Shaped intelligence is the reusable capability that helps carry recurring work burden across outputs.
Human replacementShaped intelligence carries burden, not responsibility. Human direction, judgement, approval, escalation and real-world action remain with the person.

Example

A domain-facing support capability that can carry reconstruction, sequencing, drafting, tracking, dependency and risk framing, domain-practice application, subject-context reasoning, project-state preservation, resumption and communication preparation.

Non-example

A polished answer that does not remember the work state, unresolved decisions, review basis or next-action logic.

Assessment signal

The test is whether repeated work becomes easier to resume, review and continue while preserving domain-practice standards, subject-context reasoning and human accountability.
