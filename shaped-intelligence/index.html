Concept page

Shaped intelligence carries more recurring work burden in a domain

Shaped intelligence is the reusable domain-facing capability that carries a shaped work pattern and reusable work basis into recurring work so more two-sided work burden can shift from the responsible person while human authority remains explicit. It may be developed through direct shaping or mediated shaping through AI-shaping intelligence.

Domain-facing capability

Think of AI that starts with the job’s work basis

Shaped intelligence is the reusable domain-facing capability. In plain terms, it means the AI contribution starts from a prepared work pattern and preserved work state rather than a fresh generic request.

This makes the page easier to read if you keep one question in mind: what burden no longer has to be rebuilt by the person each time the work resumes?

Domain-practice standardsMethods, standards and review expectations.
Subject-context reasoningMatter-specific context, roles and perspectives.
Work-state preservationCurrent state, unresolved matters and review status.
Work-resumption logicContinuation point, pending work and next action.

These four component terms sit within the two formal burden sides. Source basis is carried forward separately, and human authority remains explicit.

Work pattern

It carries the shaped work pattern

Shaped intelligence carries the organising structure for recurring work: the role, source relationship, domain-practice expectations, subject-context reasoning, review boundaries and output pattern.

Reusable work basis

It carries the reusable work basis forward

It carries source basis, relevant practice standards, matter-specific context, current work state and resumption logic so the work can continue rather than restart.

Definition

Shaped intelligence is the reusable domain-facing capability established through AI shaping that carries a shaped work pattern and reusable work basis into recurring work, so more two-sided work burden can shift from the responsible person.

In plain English: it is broad AI capability shaped for recurring work in a domain, carrying more of the work pattern and reusable work basis rather than starting from a fresh one-off prompt.

Why this term is needed

The value is practice context with work-state preservation and work-resumption logic. A person should not need to rebuild the whole AI work basis every time the work resumes, changes hands or receives new information.

The practical difference from ordinary prompting is that the work basis continues, instead of being rebuilt whenever the work changes, pauses or changes hands.

How it differs from adjacent ideas

Adjacent ideaDistinction
Raw model capabilityRaw capability can answer broadly. Shaped intelligence is fitted to a domain pattern, source basis, review route, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context and work-state and resumption burden.
A generated artefactA generated artefact is an output. Shaped intelligence is the reusable capability that helps carry recurring work burden across outputs.
Human replacementShaped intelligence carries burden, not responsibility. Human direction, judgement, approval, escalation and real-world action remain with the person.

Example

A domain-facing support capability that carries recurring reconstruction, sequencing, tracking, risk framing, resumption and communication-preparation work into review-ready form.

Non-example

A polished answer that does not remember the work state, unresolved decisions, review basis or next-action logic.

Assessment signal

The test is whether repeated work becomes easier to resume, review and continue because shaped intelligence carries both the domain-practice and subject-context burden, and the work-state and resumption burden, while preserving human accountability.
