Shaped intelligence

Shaped intelligence is the domain-facing reusable capability that carries recurring work burden in a work domain under human authority.

Definition. Shaped intelligence is the domain-facing reusable capability that carries more of the recurring work burden in a work domain while human direction, judgement and action remain explicit.

What makes it shaped

The capability is shaped when it preserves relevant source basis, project state, review state, domain context, unresolved decisions and next-action logic rather than treating every request as an isolated answer.

What it can carry

In the public evidence frame, shaped intelligence can carry parts of reconstruction, sequencing, drafting, tracking, dependency and risk framing, project-state preservation, resumption and communication preparation. That does not mean it owns decisions or executes real-world action.

Why it matters

The practical value is continuity. A person should not need to rebuild the whole context every time the work resumes, changes hands or receives new information.
