Integrated publication-system evidence

One publication system, three shaped-intelligence instances

Private-set paper-authoring shaped intelligence, published-set paper-authoring shaped intelligence and web-developing shaped intelligence develop one coherent AI Shaping publication system. Human direction selects one active revision at a time, while accepted states of the other relevant instances provide controlled reference context.

Plain answer

Publication co-evolution is the relationship, not another shaped intelligence

Cross-surface publication co-evolution is the controlled reciprocal-refinement process linking three separately governed direct-shaped instances. It is not a fourth shaped-intelligence instance, an autonomous coordination layer or a multi-agent system.

Each instance has its own work object, accepted state and authority boundary. The human decides which revision is active, what other accepted material may be used as reference, and whether an implication is accepted, promoted, deferred or rejected.

How cross-surface reciprocal refinement works

  1. Private material develops deeper coherence and practitioner codification.

    Strategy, architecture, principles, techniques and evaluation needs can be developed without automatically becoming public claims.

  2. Published papers formalise the public basis.

    The Category Definition and Capability Evidence papers control their formal public roles and DOI-backed release record.

  3. The website clarifies and makes the public material inspectable.

    Reader architecture, comparisons, diagrams and public-safe wording can expose clearer formulations or coherence gaps.

  4. Successful website formulations are assessed for later promotion.

    A clearer explanation may return to the appropriate paper or private material only through a separately authorised revision.

  5. The next accepted release renews the reference context.

    No change propagates automatically; human review and the receiving set's own authority still control the outcome.

Operating constraint

One active revision at a time

The three instances do not act concurrently or maintain permanent shared awareness. During an authorised revision, one instance is active and uses the latest accepted relevant states of the other sets as read-only reference context.

This sequential activation preserves separate versioning, source authority and disclosure decisions while still allowing reciprocal refinement across the publication system.

Human authority

Integration remains directed

The human selects the active work object, establishes scope, resolves conflicts, approves wording and diagrams, decides what remains private, authorises release and controls publication. Cross-surface refinement changes information flow; it does not transfer authority.

What the integration adds as evidence

The public system now exposes more than one bounded direct-shaped capability. Web-developing shaped intelligence is the easiest instance to inspect. The published paper set provides externally inspectable paper-authoring outputs. The private paper set adds a separately governed paper-authoring instance whose bounded role can be disclosed without publishing its complete evidence basis.

Together, the three instances show sustained burden-carrying behaviour across different publication surfaces and controlled integration among them. Their evidence status is not identical, and this integrated evidence does not replace the formal Capability Evidence paper's project-managing evidence package.

Public evidence boundary

This page supports bounded public-stage evaluation of the publication system. It does not disclose private papers, shaped-control files, prompts, schemas, validation mechanics or a transferable publication method. It does not establish autonomous publishing, generic paper-authoring transferability, AI-shaping-intelligence domain transfer, deployment assurance or commercial-use rights.
