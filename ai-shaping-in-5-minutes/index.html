AI shaping in 5 minutes

The simple version: shape a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern, carry the work state forward and keep human authority explicit.

Reader-layer note

This page is deliberately plain

Use this page to understand the practical problem before reading the formal terms. The glossary holds the short definitions, the papers hold the formal record and the protected-stage page holds the implementation-level boundary.

Plain example

One recognisable situation

You have a support-ticket export, a vendor email, a few shorthand notes and a stakeholder asking what is happening. Ordinary AI might summarise each item. The harder work is keeping the source basis visible, separating progress from unresolved matters, naming what needs review and preparing the next communication without pretending the human has already decided.

AI shaping aims at that second problem. It helps the work continue from a preserved, reviewable state instead of forcing the person to recreate the whole situation in a new prompt.

The problem in plain terms

General-purpose AI can produce useful output, but complex or extended work often fails because every new exchange starts without enough of the job: context, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, prior decisions, unresolved matters, review status and next action.

In practical terms, the person keeps rebuilding the long prompt. The repeated work also includes applying domain-practice expectations and subject-context reasoning, not just restating background information. That repeated reconstruction is the work burden AI shaping is designed to reduce by turning the work basis into a reusable, practice-guided and subject-aware pattern.

Ordinary AI use

The person asks for a summary, draft or analysis. The output may be helpful, but the person still has to rebuild the work context, check what is unresolved, decide what can be used, and restart the pattern again after the next pause or new input.

AI shaping

The work pattern is shaped so AI starts from the right source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, review boundaries, output expectations and preserved work state. In simple terms, AI is not being asked to improvise from a fresh prompt; it is being given a prepared work basis for the kind of job being done. The person still supplies direction, reviews, approves, escalates, communicates and takes real-world action.

Shaping

Give AI the right work pattern

Shape the role, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, review boundaries and output expectations so the AI contribution fits the work instead of behaving like a fresh one-off answer.

Resumption

Carry the work state forward

Carry what happened, what changed, what remains unresolved, what needs review and what happens next, so new information can be digested without starting again from scratch.

The core value proposition

AI shaping gives general-purpose AI a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern and work state so it can help continue complex work, not just answer a prompt. The outcome is productive AI work: grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable AI-enabled work under human direction.
