AI shaping in 5 minutes

The simple version: shape a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern, carry the work state forward and keep human authority explicit.

Next: work-burden shift Read the papers Open the glossary

The problem in one paragraph

General-purpose AI can produce useful output, but complex or extended work often fails because every new exchange starts without enough of the job: context, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, prior decisions, unresolved matters, review status and next action. That repeated reconstruction is the work burden AI shaping is designed to reduce.

Ordinary AI use

The person asks for a summary, draft or analysis. The output may be helpful, but the person still has to rebuild the work context, check what is unresolved, decide what can be used, and restart the pattern again after the next pause or new input.

AI shaping

The work pattern is shaped so AI starts from the right source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, review boundaries, output expectations and preserved work state. The person still supplies direction, reviews, approves, escalates, communicates and takes real-world action.

Shaping

Give AI the right work pattern

Shape the role, source basis, domain-practice guidance, subject-specific context, review boundaries and output expectations so the AI contribution fits the work instead of behaving like a fresh one-off answer.

Resumption

Carry the work state forward

Carry what happened, what changed, what remains unresolved, what needs review and what happens next, so new information can be digested without starting again from scratch.

The core value proposition

AI shaping gives general-purpose AI a practice-guided, subject-aware work pattern and work state so it can help continue complex work, not just answer a prompt. The outcome is productive AI work: grounded, reviewable, repeatable and resumable work under human direction.
